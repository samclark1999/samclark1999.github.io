A Little About Me

my interest in coding started when I was young. My dad worked as a programmer for a fruit warehouse company and he would share with me basic HTML syntax to create webpages. I remember creating basic sport blog webpages that were both colorful and gave information about my favorite teams and games I had watched the day before. Of course, updating the site after every game became a chore with HTML and my unpublished webpage came to a screeching hault.

Fast forward to the beginning of the pandemic in March of 2020, I started to get back into web design. Using platforms such as WordPress and Squarespace, I created webpages for a small business as well as a portfolio page for my girlfriend. I then began to think about how I could create websites for small businesses who do not have the resources or knowledge to have an online presence. This is especially important in a time where the market for online shopping has reached $851 billion dollars which equates to 44% of the market share for retail spending (digitalcommerce360, 2021).

My interest in web design has ultimately pushed me to enroll in MIT's Full Stack Coding: with MERN. This course has enlightened me to the many ways the coding environment has drastically changed from when I was a child. I want to continue to build my knowledge and leverage my marketing degree to provide webpages and marketing tools for small businesses who need an online presence!
